# PREPERATIONS TO USE CAPCUT ON LINUX (via Wine in Bottles)
1. Go to [this website](https://whitescreen.dev/black-screen) for a black screen because capcut needs to have 60% trasparency to work
2. Set your videos to have:
	* Exposure = 23
	* Contrast = 8
	* Highlight = 12
	* Shadow = 0
	* White = 15 
	* Blacks = -11
	* Brilliance = 0

For them to look similar to the exported video (reset them before export)
3. You are done!
